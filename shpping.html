Why Grey/Charcoal?
Stark white is too harsh for Soft Summer. Grey and charcoal blend with your Dusty Blue, Slate, and Navy pieces. They hide dirt better for travel. Cool-toned greys = no yellow undertones.

Avoid These

❌ Optic White — too stark, washes you out
❌ Beige/Tan — warm undertone clashes with cool palette
❌ Bright Colors — fight with your muted wardrobe
❌ Gold Accents — warm metal, use silver instead
