If it fails 2+ tests, it's probably too warm for your palette. Paper Test Hold the sneaker next to a sheet of white printer paper. Soft Summer greys should look cool/neutral against white, not creamy, beige, or yellow. Face Test Hold the shoe near your face in natural light. Does it make your skin look grey/ashy or rosy/bright? Soft Summer harmonies bring out rose in cheeks, not sallow tones. Silver Test Compare the shoe's midsole/accents to silver jewellery. Cool greys should harmonize with silver.
